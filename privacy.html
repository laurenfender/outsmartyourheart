Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

UpDate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the UpDate app ("the App").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address when you create an account.
  • Profile data: Information you enter into the App, including your self-profile ratings, standards, and ratings of people you're dating. This data is stored securely and used solely to power the App's features.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you use the App, used to improve the experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the App's features, including AI-powered analysis and strategy generation
  • Store your data securely so it persists across sessions and devices
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the App over time

AI Processing

UpDate uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate personalized dating strategies, pattern analyses, and profile content. When you use these features, relevant portions of your profile and ratings data are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not send personally identifiable information (such as your name or email) to Anthropic.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — secure data storage and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI processing for strategy and analysis features
  • Apple — payment processing via the App Store

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting the App

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@outsmartyourheart.com.

Children's Privacy

UpDate is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@outsmartyourheart.com.
