How it works

Date with intention, not just hope.

01

Be honest about yourself — and set your standard

Rate yourself truthfully on what you bring to a relationship. Then set your minimum baselines for a partner — dealbreakers, what matters most, what's nice to have. Be realistic, not aspirational.

02

Your Dating Strategy

Based on your self-profile and standard, UpDate builds you a personalized strategy — who you're really looking for, where to find them, and how to position yourself to attract them.

03

Rate your dates — and reveal your patterns

Add anyone — current or past. Rate them honestly against your standard and the dating pattern analysis reveals what you keep tolerating, what you keep missing, and where your patterns are.
