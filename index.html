How it works.

Be honest about yourself — and set your standards.

Rate yourself truthfully on what you bring to a relationship. Then set your minimum baselines for a partner — dealbreakers, what matters most, what's nice to have. Be realistic, not aspirational.

Get your dating strategy.

Based on your self-profile and standards, UpDate builds you a personalized strategy — who you're really looking for, where to find them, and how to position yourself to attract them.

Profiles built from who you actually are.

UpDate writes bios and prompt answers for Hinge, Bumble, Tinder, and Match — based on your self-profile and what you're looking for. Profiles that sound like you, not the version of you who's trying to sound interesting.

Rate someone you're dating.

Add anyone — current or past. Rate them honestly against your standards. Get an honest read on each person — what's working, what isn't, and the trade-off you're really making.

See your dating patterns.

The pattern analysis pulls together everyone you've rated and reveals what you keep tolerating, what you keep missing, and what to do differently going forward.
